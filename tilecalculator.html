üí° Pro Installation Tips

  • Layout: Dry fit tiles before setting
  • Start: Begin at room center, work outward
  • Cuts: Hide cut tiles along walls
  • Level: Check every few tiles
  • Grout: Wait 24 hours before grouting
  • Seal: Seal grout after 72 hours
