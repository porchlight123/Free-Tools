Complete Concrete Guide

Everything you need to know for a successful concrete project

üìè Thickness Guidelines

Sidewalks 4 inches
Patios 4 inches
Driveways (cars) 6 inches
Driveways (trucks) 8 inches
Garage floors 6 inches
Commercial 8+ inches

üöö Ordering Tips

  • Minimum truck delivery is usually 1 cubic yard
  • Short load fees apply under 5 yards
  • Saturday delivery costs extra
  • Have everything ready before truck arrives
  • Trucks need 14ft clearance and firm ground
  • Most trucks hold 10 cubic yards maximum

üí∞ Cost Factors

  • Ready-mix: $150-200 per cubic yard
  • Delivery fees: $50-150
  • Short load fee: $50-100
  • Weekend delivery: +$100-200
  • Pumping service: $150-300/hour
  • Bagged concrete: $5-8 per 60lb bag

üõ†Ô∏è Essential Tools

  • Wheelbarrow for moving concrete
  • Shovels and rakes for spreading
  • Screed board for leveling
  • Float for smoothing surface
  • Edger for clean edges
  • Broom for texture (non-slip)

‚è±Ô∏è Working Time

  • Initial set: 30-45 minutes
  • Final finish: 2-4 hours
  • Walk on: 24-48 hours
  • Drive on: 7 days minimum
  • Full strength: 28 days
  • Hot weather reduces working time

üå°Ô∏è Weather Considerations

  • Ideal temp: 50-75¬∞F
  • Too hot: Concrete sets too fast
  • Too cold: Use cold weather mix
  • Never pour on frozen ground
  • Cover if rain expected
  • Keep moist for 7 days to cure
